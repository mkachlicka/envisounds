Magdalena Kachlicka

Magdalena currently works as a Postdoctoral Researcher at Birkbeck University. Her research focuses on categorization, attention and individual differences in perception within the auditory world - speech, music, and environmental sounds. You can find out more about her work on her website.

×

Dr Jasper van den Bosch

Jasper is a Research Fellow at University of Leeds and a co-founder of Meadows Research. He is interested in how the brain represents the world through different sensory modalities.

×

Prof Jian Kang

Jian is a Professor of Acoustics and Soundscape at Bartlett School, Environment, Energy & Resources Faculty of the Built Environment.

×

Prof Fred Dick

Fred is a Professor of Auditory Cognitive Neuroscience in the Department of Experimental Psychology at UCL and Director of the Birkbeck/UCL Centre for NeuroImaging (BUCNI). He is also a co-director of the Auditory Langauge, Perception, Hearing and Attention Lab.
